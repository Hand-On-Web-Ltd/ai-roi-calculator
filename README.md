# AI ROI Calculator

A simple web calculator that helps businesses figure out how much they could save by automating repetitive tasks with AI.

Plug in your numbers — staff count, hours wasted on manual work, hourly rates — and see what automation could save you per month and per year.

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser (no server needed)
2. Fill in your business details
3. Hit calculate
4. See your potential savings

## What It Calculates

- **Monthly savings** based on automating a percentage of repetitive work
- **Yearly savings** projection
- **Break-even estimate** for typical AI automation projects

The default automation rate is 60% — most businesses can automate about 60% of their repetitive admin tasks with the right setup.

## Try It

Just download and open `index.html`. That's it. No dependencies, no build step, no npm install.

## Tech

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Zero dependencies. Works offline.
